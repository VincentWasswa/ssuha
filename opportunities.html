Employment

At SSUHA we believe everyone needs an opportunity to express his abilitiesand talent. Thats why we dont discriminate anyone whether according to race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation or background. Everyone is given equal opportunity and its up to you to meet our expectations.

When it comes to success, there is rarely ever a time when luck comes into play. Hard work however, always comes into play. Hard work does not offer a guarantee of success but without it there is a guarantee, the guarantee of failure. The harder you work, the luckier you get. The harder you work, the richer you get. The harder you work, the greater your success.

Internship

We offer internship opportunities to undergraduates trying to kickstart thier careers at most levels of education check us out regularly for updates on available opportunities at SSUHA, great to kickstart your carrer.

Volunteer

You are welcome to work as a volunteer in our organisation.We shall be happy to work with you as part of the SSUHA team to carry on with our mission.
